AI Walk-to-Earn Harlem

Redesigning Harlem Plaza with AI, Movement, and Community Rewards

Home

This project redesigns a Harlem plaza into an interactive walking space where people earn rewards while exploring the area.

About the Project

The goal is to increase walking, improve safety, and support local businesses using an AI-powered reward system.

The Problem

The plaza currently lacks activity, incentives, and safety features. People pass through but don’t stay or engage.

Site Analysis

Montefiore Square shows limited seating, weak pedestrian flow, and low interaction with nearby businesses.

Design Options

Multiple AI-generated concepts were created, including green plazas, social spaces, and reward-based walking zones.

Final Proposal

The final design includes smart walking paths, seating areas, lighting, and reward checkpoints connected to local businesses.

How It Works

Users walk → AI verifies movement → rewards are given through a mobile app → businesses benefit from increased traffic.

AI System

The system uses GPS tracking, activity detection, and AI recommendations to guide users through safe and active routes.

Reflection

AI helped generate ideas and visuals, but human decisions shaped the final design, layout, and user experience.
